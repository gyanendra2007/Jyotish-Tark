Refund Policy

At Jyotish Tark, customer satisfaction is our priority. Please review our refund policy below.

Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds are considered in the following cases:

  • Technical issues preventing delivery of your report
  • Duplicate charges for the same service
  • Service not rendered as described
  • Cancellation within the allowed timeframe

Non-Refundable Services

The following are generally not eligible for refunds:

  • Reports that have been delivered successfully
  • Change of mind after receiving the service
  • Disagreement with astrological predictions
  • Consultations that have already taken place

Refund Process

If eligible for a refund:

  • Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days
  • Amount will be credited to the original payment method
  • You will receive confirmation email once processed
