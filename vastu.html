Common questions about Vastu Shastra and our consultation process.

What is Vastu Shastra and how does it work?

Vastu Shastra is an ancient Indian science of architecture and spaces that aims to create harmonious environments by balancing the five elements (earth, water, fire, air, and space). It works by aligning the energies of a space with natural forces to promote health, wealth, and happiness.

How long does it take to see results from Vastu changes?

While some changes may show immediate effects, most Vastu corrections take 2-3 months to manifest noticeable results. The timeframe can vary based on the specific changes implemented and how consistently they are maintained.

Can Vastu help with specific problems like financial issues or health concerns?

Yes, Vastu offers specific solutions for different life areas. For financial improvement, we focus on the north direction and wealth corners. For health issues, we analyze the kitchen, bedroom, and bathroom placements along with the overall energy flow of the home.

Do I need to make structural changes to my home/office for Vastu compliance?

Not necessarily. While major structural changes can have significant impact, many Vastu defects can be corrected through simple remedies like strategic placement of elements, colors, mirrors, plants, and symbolic objects. We always suggest the least invasive solutions first.
