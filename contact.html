Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our astrology services and consultations.

How soon can I get a consultation?

We typically respond within 24 hours to schedule a consultation. Emergency consultations may be available upon request.

What information do I need to provide for a Kundali reading?

For an accurate Kundali reading, we need your exact date of birth, time of birth, and place of birth. The more precise the information, the more accurate the reading.

Do you provide services in Hindi?

Yes, all our consultations and reports are available in both Hindi and English based on your preference.

Can I get a consultation for someone else?

Yes, you can request a consultation for family members or friends, but you will need their birth details and preferably their consent for the reading.
