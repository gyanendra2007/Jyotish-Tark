What is Numerology?

Numerology is an ancient metaphysical science that studies the mystical relationship between numbers and life events. It's based on the idea that the universe is a system that can be broken down into its basic elements - numbers.

By analyzing the numbers derived from your birth date and name, numerology reveals your innate talents, personality traits, challenges, and ultimate life purpose. It's like a cosmic blueprint that guides you toward self-discovery and fulfillment.

The most important number in your numerology chart is your Life Path Number, which is calculated from your complete birth date. This number represents the path you'll walk in this lifetime and reveals your natural abilities and challenges.

How It Works

  1. Write down your complete birth date (DD/MM/YYYY format)
  2. Add all the digits together (day + month + year)
  3. Reduce to a single digit by adding the digits of the sum together
  4. Exception for Master Numbers - If you get 11, 22, or 33, these are not reduced further as they carry special vibrations
  5. Discover your unique Life Path Number meaning and how it influences your life journey

Example Calculation

Birth Date: 15/04/1990

Day: 1+5 = 6 | Month: 0+4 = 4 | Year: 1+9+9+0 = 19 → 1+9 = 10 → 1+0 = 1

Total: 6 + 4 + 1 = 11 (Master Number - not reduced further)

Numerology FAQs

Q: What is a Life Path Number?

A: Your Life Path Number is the most important number in your numerology chart. It's calculated from your birth date and reveals your natural talents, challenges, and the path you're meant to walk in this lifetime.

Q: How accurate is numerology?

A: Numerology has been practiced for thousands of years across many cultures. While not a exact science, many people find remarkable accuracy in how their numbers describe their personality and life experiences.

Q: What are Master Numbers?

A: Master Numbers (11, 22, and 33) are not reduced to a single digit as they carry higher spiritual vibrations and greater potential, but also greater challenges and responsibilities.

Q: Can numerology predict my future?

A: Numerology doesn't predict specific events but reveals your innate tendencies, strengths, and challenges. It provides guidance on how to navigate your life path more effectively.

Q: How does name numerology work?

A: Name numerology calculates the numerical value of the letters in your name to reveal your Expression Number (personality others see) and Soul Urge Number (your inner desires and motivations).
