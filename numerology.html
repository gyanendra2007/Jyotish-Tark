The Power of Numbers

What is Numerology?

Numerology is the mystical study of numbers and their influence on human life. It reveals that your birth date and name contain vibrational patterns that shape your personality, talents, and life path.

By calculating your Life Path Number (derived from your birth date), you can gain profound insights into your innate strengths, challenges, and soul's purpose in this lifetime.

How It Works

  1. Write down your complete birth date (DD/MM/YYYY)
  2. Add all the digits together
  3. Reduce to a single digit (except Master Numbers 11 & 22)
  4. Discover your unique Life Path Number meaning

Example: Birth Date - 15/04/1990

1+5 + 0+4 + 1+9+9+0 = 29 → 2+9 = 11 (Master Number)
