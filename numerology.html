What is Numerology?

Numerology is an ancient metaphysical science that studies the mystical relationship between numbers and life events. It's based on the idea that the universe is a system that can be broken down into its basic elements - numbers.

By analyzing the numbers derived from your birth date and name, numerology reveals your innate talents, personality traits, challenges, and ultimate life purpose. It's like a cosmic blueprint that guides you toward self-discovery and fulfillment.

The most important number in your numerology chart is your Life Path Number, which is calculated from your complete birth date. This number represents the path you'll walk in this lifetime and reveals your natural abilities and challenges.

How It Works

  1. Write down your complete birth date (DD/MM/YYYY format)
  2. Add all the digits together (day + month + year)
  3. Reduce to a single digit by adding the digits of the sum together
  4. Exception for Master Numbers - If you get 11, 22, or 33, these are not reduced further as they carry special vibrations
  5. Discover your unique Life Path Number meaning and how it influences your life journey

Example Calculation

Birth Date: 15/04/1990

Day: 1+5 = 6 | Month: 0+4 = 4 | Year: 1+9+9+0 = 19 → 1+9 = 10 → 1+0 = 1

Total: 6 + 4 + 1 = 11 (Master Number - not reduced further)

Numerology FAQs

Q: What is a Life Path Number in Numerology?

A: Your Life Path Number is the most important number in Numerology. It is calculated from your date of birth and reveals your personality traits, strengths, challenges, and life purpose. For example, Life Path Number 1 shows leadership, while 7 shows spirituality.

Q: How accurate is Numerology?

A: Numerology has been used for thousands of years in Indian अंक ज्योतिष and Western systems. While it is not an exact science, many people find surprising accuracy in predictions related to career, love, marriage, and destiny numbers.

Q: What are Master Numbers in Numerology?

A: Master Numbers (11, 22, 33) are powerful numbers in अंक ज्योतिष. They are not reduced to a single digit and indicate high spiritual energy, intuition, and responsibility. Example: Master Number 11 is called the “Spiritual Teacher.”

Q: Can Numerology predict my future?

A: Numerology does not predict exact events but shows the energies and patterns in your life. For example, your Personal Year Number can indicate whether the year will be good for career, love, or new beginnings.

Q: How does Name Numerology work?

A: Name Numerology calculates the numbers behind your name using Pythagorean or Chaldean methods. It reveals your Expression Number (how the world sees you) and Soul Urge Number (your inner desires). Correcting your name vibrations can attract success and positivity.

Q: How to calculate Numerology from Date of Birth?

A: To calculate your Life Path Number, add all the digits of your birth date until you reach a single digit (or Master Number). Example: 14 July 1995 → 1+4+7+1+9+9+5 = 36 → 3+6 = 9. Life Path Number = 9.

Q: Which number is lucky for me in Numerology?

A: Your lucky number is usually your Life Path Number or Destiny Number. For example, if your Life Path is 5, then numbers related to 5 (like 14, 23, 32) bring good fortune. Many people also use Name Numerology to find lucky numbers for business and marriage.
